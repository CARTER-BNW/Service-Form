About Me

I’m a Melbourne based photographer with over 15 years of experience behind the camera, capturing real moments, real people, and real stories.

My work is grounded in observation and timing. I don’t just take photos, I read the scene, anticipate moments, and capture them in a way that feels natural and honest.

I shoot with a mix of documentary and creative style. While I’m known for strong black and white work, I also deliver clean, vibrant colour images depending on the project.
