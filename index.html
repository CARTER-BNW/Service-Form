About Me

I’m a Melbourne based photographer with over 15 years of experience behind the camera, capturing real moments, real people, and real stories.

My work is grounded in observation and timing. I don’t just take photos, I read the scene, anticipate moments, and capture them in a way that feels natural and honest. Whether it’s a busy street, a quiet portrait, or a high energy event, I focus on creating images that feel alive and authentic.

I shoot with a mix of documentary and creative style. While I’m known for strong black and white work, I also deliver clean, vibrant colour images depending on the project and what suits the story best. Every photo is carefully edited from RAW to ensure a polished and professional result.

My approach is simple. Make people feel comfortable, stay present, and let moments unfold. That’s where the best images come from.
